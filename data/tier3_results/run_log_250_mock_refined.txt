     signature. An insider threat shows identity STABLE + data_behavior DRIFTING.

     methods cannot distinguish which behavioral DIMENSION is changing.

     investigation scenarios, the same embeddings reveal different threats.
     'apt_hunt' context (network=0.40) amplifies C2 signals that uniform
     weighting dilutes. 'insider_investigation' (data=0.40) amplifies exfil.

  3. VELOCITY VECTORS: Full 1536-d velocity captures not just HOW MUCH
     an entity drifted, but the exact DIRECTION and ACCELERATION. An entity
     accelerating toward threat-space is a stronger signal than slow drift.

  4. RELATIONSHIP EMBEDDINGS: UserDevice Hadamard products capture interaction
     patterns. When a C2 beacon changes how a user uses their device, the
     relationship vector drifts even if neither entity individually changes.
